COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING VADAKARA

The College of Engineering Vadakara (CEV) is a government-run engineering college in Kerala, established in 1999 as the first institution under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE) . Situated on a 27-acre, green hilltop campus called "Kakoramala" in the Maniyur village of Kozhikode district, the college is affiliated with the A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and accredited by NAAC. CEV offers undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (MCA) programs in various engineering disciplines, with admission to B.Tech courses primarily based on the KEAM entrance exam. The campus provides facilities such as a central library, computing resources, Wi-Fi connectivity, hostels, a canteen, and a dedicated training and placement cell that has attracted recruiters like Infosys and Wipro.
